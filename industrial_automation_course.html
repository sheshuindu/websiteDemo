Description

Industrial automation drives today’s industrial world. Sensors, drivers and controllers are integrated for providing effective automation in industries. A programmable logic controller (PLC) is an industrial digital computer which has been designed and adapted for automating an industry. Automating can make industries have increased quality, accuracy and precision to industrial processes and thereby more productive. The need for automation will always increase. Due to the high amount of vacancies across manufacturing processes Job opportunities in the automation industry are vast. Automation engineers are responsible for design, programming, simulation and testing of automated machinery and processes in an industry.

Advanced PLC Brands - Allen Bradley, Siemens, Omron, Mitsubishi Electric, Delta, Schneider Electric, Keyence & TIA.
