Engineering Project

Description

The engineering project is a human activity that can have broadly varying scope, all the way from the design, manufacturing, distribution, and recycling of a paper clip, to the design, construction, management, and eventual decommissioning of a mass transit system. Engineering project management is focused on managing engineering projects. An example may be a project to design a new office building. One key difference is that engineering project management requires the management of all aspects of the project. Not just management of the engineers or the engineering work.
