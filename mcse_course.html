Description

Softlogic’s MCSE (Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert) certification imparts the skills required to form extensive server infrastructure solutions, and present your ability to competently run a modern and intelligent datacentre. This is done through specialization in data identity management, systems management, storage, virtualization, and networking. Softlogic has a holistic training program to guide you in the learning of MCSE-related concepts. This course concentrates on enhancing your ability to design and build technology solutions in the cloud and on premises. Our curriculum focuses on validating your skills in the domain of identity management, virtualization, systems management, storage and networking.
